    Scope

    This project started as a web version of a growing collection of notes on various areas of mathematics and physics I started writing on 2018.

    Although I plan to eventually convert all of them into web form as well as keep expanding and polishing their content here, currently this project's scope is a bit more restricted, aiming instead to essentially become a Stacks Project for category theory.

    Here we detail what content is planned to be first included and developed in this project.

    1. Elementary category theory, including e.g the basics of categories and functors, adjunctions, co/limits, co/ends, Kan extensions, monads, algebraic theories, operads, and monoidal categories.
    2. A comprehensive and exhaustive account of fibred, enriched, and internal category theory.
    3. A comprehensive account of topos theory, as developed in Johnstone's Sketches of an Elephant.
    4. An account of abelian categories and homological algebra.
    5. A comprehensive account of bicategories, bilimits, biadjunctions, etc.
    6. A comprehensive account of the theory of double categories.
    7. A comprehensive account of the theory of model categories.
    8. A comprehensive and exhaustive account of $\infty$-categories.

    A signifcant part of the material outlined above has already been completed, but exists only in PDF form at the moment.
